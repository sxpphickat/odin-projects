LASAGNA

Lasagna is a delicious Italian pasta dish made with layers of flat lasagna noodles

Lasagna is a hearty and comforting dish that's perfect for feeding a crowd or for a cozy family dinner. It's often served with a side salad and garlic bread. You can also customize it by adding vegetables like spinach or mushrooms to the layers or making it vegetarian by omitting the meat. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!

ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or pork
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

half of the steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until it's browned and crumbled. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, allowing it to thicken.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, chopped parsley (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well.
